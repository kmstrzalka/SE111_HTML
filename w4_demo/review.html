Review of the movie Beetlejuice(1988)

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

one of if not my most favorite moive from the late 80's, a must watch if you haven't. For being the 1990 winner of the Saturn Award, this movie went though alot of changes and almost wasn't called beetlejuice but "Scared Sheetless".

Trivia about movie title

  • trivia #1 Tim Burton feared the "Day-O" sequence wouldn't go over well, since in his opinion it wasn't very funny. He turned out to be wrong: audiences loved it and think of it as one of the film's most iconic scenes.
  • trivia #2 The film was the first DVD sent out by Netflix in 1998.
  • trivia #3 The studio originally wanted to call the film "House Ghosts." As a joke, Tim Burton suggested the name "Scared Sheetless" and was horrified when the studio actually considered using it.
